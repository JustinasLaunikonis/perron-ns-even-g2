# Release notes

Entered per supported language in the Even Hub dashboard at submission.
For the first version, these describe what the app does (not "initial release").

## en — v0.1.4

Under-the-hood maintenance: updated to the latest Even Hub SDK and tidied up the
codebase. No changes to how the app works.

## en — v0.1.3

Your favorites and saved journeys now stick around: they no longer disappear
when you close and reopen the app.

## en — v0.1.2

Layout fixes for smaller phone screens: the favorites "Add" button and the
station search suggestions now stay neatly inside their cards.

## en — v0.1.1

You can now plan ahead: tap the time box to pick a departure *or* arrival time and
a day, or jump back to "now" with one tap. Plus a smaller, faster app download.

## en — v0.1.0

Plan NS (Dutch Railways) journeys from your glasses. Glance at the lens for live
departure times, delays, platforms and transfers; set up routes, favorites and a
recent-journey history on your phone and pick them with a tap or swipe.
